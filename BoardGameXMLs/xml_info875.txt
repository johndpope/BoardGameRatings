But beware, the only thing you own is what is on your transporters, and anyone can use any production facility, or pick up any goods left behind.&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;In more detail, this massive set of pieces looks more like a modular game kit than anything else. The tiles are laid out in whatever scenario you wish to play, and then the clear plastic is taped over the top to secure the entire board. The idea is that your transportation units (at first, a fleet of donkeys) travel about and pick items that part produced. However, the only thing that you own is that which is carried by your transports. So you might have a nice, shiny, new truck factory or a gold-filled mine, but anyone can use it or take it, if they collect the necessary components and can transport them to the factory.
