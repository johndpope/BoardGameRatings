On your turn, you roll two dice for movement, resulting in between 2 and 12 movement points to allocate.  Infantry moves 1 per pip, cavalry and artillery 2 per pip (a one-square move still consumes 1 pip).  Mountains force pieces to pause (they must stop until the next turn).  Railroads link stations and can be used to accelerate movement (one station per pip).&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;Combat is handled by lining pieces up (horizontally or vertically) to face (or flank) an enemy column.
