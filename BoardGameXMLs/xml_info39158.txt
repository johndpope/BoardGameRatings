At the start of the game, one player is secretly assigned the role of Opener, but no one - not event the player who is the Opener - knows who the Opener is. A Haunting card comes into play when another player probes your destiny pile and finds a Haunting card.&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;Haunted Destinies uses a tiled board system similar to Betrayal at the House on the Hill, but each tile edge indicates the minimum number of movement points need to leave through that edge.
