Play consists of being dealt (4) randomized playing cards; followed by the clockwise execution of various actions in a race to match the goal card in the center. This means that each players goal is unique to their perspective of the goal card. After the cards are dealt, each players stands them keeping them secret. Each round consists of checking for matches, and execution of actions until one player claims victory. Players begin with (4) action tokens and lose one for every match.
