The game will be supported by a range of painted and assembled 1/1000 miniatures.&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;Players will choose their maneuver cards in secret, then reveal them all at the same moment. The first series of Sails of Glory will be set in the Napoleonic Age.
