Each hero has variable melee, magic and speed scores, as well as a unique ability. An alternate win goal is as the Dungeonlord if all the other heroes are defeated except yours.&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;Vault of the Fiends features a variety of new heroes.  Elf Assassin, Human Beastmaster, Drakan Sentinal, Ork Shaman, Dwarf Runecaster, and Gnome Illusionist.&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;Each turn you may:&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;     Play monsters, traps and other bad events on opponents&lt;br/&gt;     Build a dungeon&lt;br/&gt;     Move around the dungeon with your hero (collect Glory and Peril resources)&lt;br/&gt;     Play abilities and treasures for yourself&lt;br/&gt;     Complete a Quest&lt;br/&gt;     Discard an unwanted card&lt;br/&gt;     Fill your hand to 5 cards&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;This game is similar to Wiz-War where you race in a dungeon to complete your objectives.  This dungeon is created by the laying down of Map cards. You collect Glory and Peril as you move through the dungeon.  Glory buys cards that help your hero such as abilities and treasures, but your opponent spends your Peril against you to play monsters, traps and other unpleasant stuff.&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;Combat is similar to Talisman. Most of the fun comes from the theme, and surprising combination of events that occur.  Dungeoneer is likely to appeal to those who enjoy a classic dungeon crawl and RPG like action.&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;Vault of the Fiends features a few new mechanics that should serve to enhance the game play which players of Tomb of the Lich Lord will be familiar with.
