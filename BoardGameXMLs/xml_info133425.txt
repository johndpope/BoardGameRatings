Shuffle well and deal equally among the players who must examine their cards; if a player holds any complete fours, lay these on the table.&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;The player on the right of the dealer asks any player if he holds a card needed to complete a set of four. The enquiring player must always have at least one of the set in their hand before asking. If the player asked holds the card in question, he must surrender it. The player who was succesful may continue to ask the one who has surrendered a card for a further card. The moment that player cannot supply the wanted card, he becomes the one to ask any player for a card.&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;If a completed Four is obtained, the succesful player should lay it on the table and that set is now safe.
