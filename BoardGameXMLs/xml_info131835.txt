The goal of Boss Monster is to be the first Boss to amass ten Souls, which are gained when a Hero is lured and defeated &amp;mdash; but a player can lose if his Boss takes five Wounds from Heroes who survive his dungeon.&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;Playing Boss Monster requires you to juggle two competing priorities: the need to lure Heroes at a faster rate than your opponents, and the need to kill those Heroes before they reach your Boss.
