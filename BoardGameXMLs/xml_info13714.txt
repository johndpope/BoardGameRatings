It is essentially a Tic-Tac-Toe game in a 4x4x4 cube.&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;The cube needs to be emptied before start. Each player choose a color of their pieces and take turn to place their pieces in vacant positions, one piece at a time. The player wins who first gets four in a row of his own pieces - either horizontal, vertical or diagonal. A player cannot remove or replace pieces that have already been placed.
