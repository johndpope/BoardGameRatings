The same images or numbers are scattered on different cards. A set of tiles or small cards is used to call out which image/number the players should cover up with a chip. The first to get one row complete wins a round. Many people can play at once; one older game was designed for large groups of up to 48 players.&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;There are many picture lotto sets published for young children, often with themes (baby animal lotto, Monsters, Inc. lotto, etc.).
