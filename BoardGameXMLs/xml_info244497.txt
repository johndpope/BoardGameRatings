Both players select their Athlete card &amp;amp; their respective tactics card deck&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;3. Both Player shuffle their Tactics Deck and place it at the right side of their Athlete card.&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;4.
