Each player then will receive a total of 6 ingredient cards for them to play.&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;In shopping phase, each player will take an ingredient card in their hand and put it face down in front of them. This card will become a new hand for cooking the menu. The fun coming when drafting, all player must not see the card they already put in front of them face down. The player will cook until they can't cook any menu. When this happen, discard the remaining ingredient to discard pile and the turn cooking will be pass to left player. After all player has their turn to cook over, the next round will be start, open new menu card so the size of menu queue will be up each round up to 6 cards open, pass priority cooking to player clockwise, and then give each player 6 new ingredient cards. Then the phase will be start over from shopping.&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;The game end when all menu card is already cooked or discarded.
