			<description>WORDOS is an outrageously quirky and interactive tabletop card game.&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;There are category cards and letter cards. Use the letter cards to create and spell any answer you want in reference to the category card. Be quick. Be smart. Be funny. The letters are points that help you move around the board to victory!&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;Gameplay - To start the game, one person flips a 'category' card. Then, everyone at the table flips over a 'letter' card from the stack. You now must think of an answer (in your head) to the category card that starts with the letter you flipped over. It can be one word, two words, or even a sentence. Once you have decided your answer, pick up the remaining number of letter cards you need to complete your answer. Once everyone has done this gameplay now begins. The person that flipped over the category is now the 'caller' and must dictate the pace of play. Everytime the caller says 'GO' everyone must discard a letter card that they do not need to spell their answer. Once discarded, you can steal anyone's discarded letter card by yelling out that letter. If you do not need any of the discarded letter cards, you pick up a card from the stack. If there is a tie, a tiebreaker occurs by someone flipping over a letter card and the players that tied must yell out a word that starts with that letter. Once a player has completed their answer, they say done and show the table. They get 1 point for every letter they used in their answer. The number of people that will finish their answer depends on the number of people playing. Half the amount of players get to finish (Example: 4 players, 2 players get to finish. 5 players is 2 players, 6 players is 3 players and so on..) Once the amount of players finish their answer the round is over. The first to make it to 30 points wins!&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;</description>
