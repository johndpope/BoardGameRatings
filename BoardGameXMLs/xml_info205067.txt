The Game Master crafts a Quest(s) for the players to complete. The info section on our website provides advice on how to create Quests.  &lt;br/&gt;2. Players create their very own, customizeable characters by drawing cards from the Role Deck.&lt;br/&gt;3. Players adventure through different Locations and encounter various Adversaries on their way to complete the Game Master's Quest(s).&lt;br/&gt;4. Players are to use their creativity and imagination in order to deal with a the situations they come across until they accomplish their given Quest(s).&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;Game Master - The Game Master's job is to weave together a fantasy setting for the players to explore:&lt;br/&gt;- Craft an engaging Quest(s) for the game (e.g. Hunting down a powerful Adversary, finding a unique Item, etc).&lt;br/&gt;- Decide players' fates using character stats and die rolls (die roll guidelines are described on the included Info Cards as well as the Info section of our website).&lt;br/&gt;- Tell a story as players advance towards their Quest goal.&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;Players - In order to complete the Game Master's Quest(s), players utilize their imaginations to overcome any obstacles in their paths:&lt;br/&gt;- Create their own unique character through drawing Role Cards.&lt;br/&gt;- Work together to accomplish the Game Master's Quest(s).&lt;br/&gt;- Respond to situations and obstacles by coming up with creative solutions.&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;~~~~~~~~~&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;SOME ADVICE&lt;br/&gt;1 - Deck Quest is supposed to be flexible.
