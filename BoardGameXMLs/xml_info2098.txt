What makes this game different is there are two areas to fight/control and the artwork on the cards.&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;The story, Heaven has fallen and the Angels trapped on Earth are trying to use the Matrix (internet) to open a gateway into Heaven.&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;There are six basic types of cards.&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;Locations (lands) that open (tap) to provide aura (mana) or store tau (victory points.)
